Kestrel Blackmore talks with Dave Rael about priorities, family, impact, humanity, and leading teams

Kestrel Blackmore is the founder of ManagingSmartly.com where he helps software developers become managers. He hosts a weekly management podcast where he interviews developers, who have already made the leap into management, to gain insight on the mistakes and successes they have made along the way.
