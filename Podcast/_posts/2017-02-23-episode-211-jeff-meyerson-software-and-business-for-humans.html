Jeff Meyerson talks with Dave Rael about podcasting, learning and listening, gratitude, and mentors

Jeff Meyerson is the host of Software Engineering Daily, a podcast about the world through the lens of software. He is also the creator of Adforprize and a musician who writes music as The Prion, which can be found on Spotify and SoundCloud.
