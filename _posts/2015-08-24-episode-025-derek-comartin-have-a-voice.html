Derek Comartin talks with Dave Rael about understanding business domains, access to domain experts, and finding your voice

Derek Comartin is software developer with over 16 years of experience that span product development, professional services and in-house enterprise development. He's written software for a variety of business domains such as consumer goods, distribution, transportation, manufacturing, and accounting. He is currently Director of Engineering at Full Circle TMS.
