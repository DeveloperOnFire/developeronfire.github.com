Jonathan's top 3 tips for delivering more value:

1. Never seperate yourself from the importance of relationships - "Relationships are the fundamental reason why computing matters in the first place"
2. Think first about what is ideal (seek identification of the best solution, not the best possible solution) - break limitations
3. Remember that technology and ideas are expendable
