Nathan Carroll talks with Dave Rael about a priority for family, dedication to physical condition, rewards of software, and learning from Budhism

Nathan Carroll has been developing software in .NET since 1.0. He is the author of the open source frameworks BattleAxe, and ULFBERHT.js. He is a workout fanatic and has built several fitness web sites over the past 8 that have evolved into his current web site Boost.Training. He also enjoys reading, cooking, and hiking with family.
